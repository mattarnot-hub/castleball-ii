CASTLEBALL II STADIUM SIM · BAT & FIELD
◂ RULES CONFIGURATOR

📱 BAT (touch)

Use the on-screen ◂ / ▸ buttons to slide the bat and the HIT button to swing (the first HIT also throws the pitch). You can still drag to aim if you prefer.

📱 FIELD (touch)

Drag the nearest fielder onto the ball, then tap the base you want the throw to go to — 2nd · 3rd · 1st · home. Outfielders can double-tap the net at the bottom to smash the ball home (retires the side if a runner is off-base).

⌨ KEYBOARD

SPACE pitch / swing · ◂▸ aim the bat. Field with the arrows; every defender throws ◂3rd ▴2nd ▸1st ▾home. Outfielders double-tap ▾ to smash the ball home into the net.

RULES

Balls · Strikes · Outs · 9 innings, Team A vs Team B. Ball outside the net = ball (4 = walk); into the net = strike (+50 defence).
